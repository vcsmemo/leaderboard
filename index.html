Qualification Criteria

To qualify on this list, startups must have:

  • over $5MM ARR (run rate)
  • under 50 employees
  • under 5 years old

Exceptions can be made for prominent startups with more than $1MM ARR/employee

Note: Numbers are snapshot-in-time figures, as startup companies are constantly growing and evolving
